Khalid ibn al-Walid(خالد بن الوليد بن المغيرة المخزومي)

The man who was also called by Sayf Allah (The sword of Allah) thanks to his intelligence and bravery on the battlefield which had led Muslims to win many battles.

Illustration of Khaled ibn al-Walid on the battlefield

Khaled ibn al-Walid was born in the 6th century CE in Mecca, pre-Islamic Arabia and died in 642.

Here's a time line of Khaled Ibn al-Walid's battles that he led and of course have won:

  • 629 - Battle of Mu'ta
  • 629 or 630 - Conquest of Mecca
  • 630 - Battle of Hunayn
      • Ridda wars
    • 632 - Battle of Buzakha
    • 633 - Battle of Aqraba
        • Early campaigns in Iraq
    • 633 - Battle of Dhat al-Salasil
    • 633 - Battle of Nahr al-Mar'a
    • 633 - Battle of Ullays
    • 633 - Battle of Walaja
    • 633 - Capture of al-Hira
    • 633 - BSiege of Anbar
    • 633 - Siege of Ayn al-Tamr
    • 634 - Battle of Firaz

      • And other more and more battles
